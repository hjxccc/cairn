# Pitfalls ledger

> One line per pitfall: `- YYYY-MM-DD <what bit you> → <countermeasure> [keywords]`.
> Log it the moment it bites — don't batch.
> Append-only applies to *new* pitfalls: if a countermeasure changes, **edit the
> original line in place** (append `rev:MM-DD`) — never leave old and new advice coexisting.
> Recurring / high-damage pitfalls graduate into an agent rule (CLAUDE.md/AGENTS.md)
> or a pre-check in the relevant SOP.
